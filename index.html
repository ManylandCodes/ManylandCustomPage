Thanks for being in Manyland! What's your question or feedback?

  • How can I find help?

    Here's our general help page. We also have a help board where you can ask questions. To get the help board, please visit Helptown. In the first hut, you'll find a container you can collect. In it there's a help board for you to post a new thread in.

  • I'm locked/ banned, what do I do?

    Oops, you may have received too many flag-reports by other long-term manyzens and are temporarily locked. The first lock fades out after some time, the second lock takes longer, and sometimes the first lock is also extended in time. Please check the etiquette and terms to find out more about what might have been the reasons for the lock.

    Please note typical reasons for flags-reports include

    • being disrespectful or rude to others
    • swearing a lot in public places
    • killing others outside of a voluntary game
    • using multiple accounts
    • recreations of copyrighted or trademarked characters
    • posting private information or account-related information on boards
    • deleting good structures or placing bad structures
    • posting irrelevant content to boards
    • being below 13 years of age
    • very offensive creations
    • and other things that go against the etiquette, the writable etiquette, or the terms

  • I have an issue with another person

    If you feel someone's treating you wrongly, or they do something against the etiquette or terms, please feel free to flag-report them. To do so, click them and then click the flag button. If you write a flag report, please be as specific as possible. Thanks!

  • Manyland doesn't load for me

    One common cause of loading issues is the lack of websockets. To see if you have websockets, please visit this page and check if all checkmarks are green. If there's any checkmark that's not green and you don't know about websockets, please talk to your system administrator, parent, or other tech person nearby to ask how to enable websockets for all ports.

    If you get all green checkmarks above and it looks like another loading issue, please email us and tell us: What browser and operating system you have; What specifically the load page or error says; Any other information you can think of that might be relevant. Thanks!

  • Manyland shows a failwhale error

    We automatically log errors and try to fix them. If there's a repeated error, please post a new thread on the Failwhale board, describing the issue. To get the Failwhale board, please visit Helptown. In the first hut, you'll find a container you can collect, and there's a Failwhale hunt board where you can add threads to.

  • I'm being logged in as someone else

    If you're on a shared computer and you're auto-logged-in as someone else, please try this:

    1. Log out of Manyland (click on the avatar, then click logout, then click the link)
    2. Go to google.com and log out of Google, if logged in
    3. Go to facebook.com and log out of Facebook, if logged in
    4. Go to amazon.com and log out of Amazon, if logged in
    5. Now go back to Manyland and hit the Login button. You should now be asked for your credentials. (When logging out again later, make sure you also log out of Google/ Facebook/ Amazon.)

  • I've seen a copyright issue.

    If you see a pixel-by-pixel clone or problematic, non-Fair-Use recreations of trademarked characters or similar issues, please open the item's context menu (on desktop, right-click; on tablets, long-tap) and flag it. If you are a copyright owner with concerns, please see this page.
